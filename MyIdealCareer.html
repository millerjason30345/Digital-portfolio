This is a typical radio mast featuring equiptment for the MNOs (mobile network operators) such as panel antennas, microwave dishes that send and receive data from other radio sites and RRUs (remote radio head) that amplifies the outgoing radio signals. they come in many forms, this example is a lattice mast
These engineers are contracted by an MNO to install, replace or upgrade antennas, its equptment as well as the antenna frame, as seen in the picture it can be a challenging job as work is conducted at a great height and in many weather forms.
You may see these engineers working up telecom poles or in the green boxes dotted along the street. this role invloves maintanence, installing and upgrading ISP infrastructure such as fibre optic cables, to working in telephone exchanges and data centres.
Similar to the above, this would invlove working with and designing electrical power systems, its circuits, components and diagnosing and fixing problems
