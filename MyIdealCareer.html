My ideal career would be one in the engineering sector, specifically telecommunications and computer networks. Other engineering roles I have considered pursuing are in the electrical industry.

More information can be found on my CV.

Mast Rigging

Similar to a network engineer, Tower Riggers are contracted by a mobile operating company to install, test, maintain, and replace radio antennas, its cabling, and other equipment, as well as build the mast itself.

Telecommunications Engineer

You may see these engineers working in street fiber cabinets around the country; the job involves fixing outages/damage and routine maintenance/replacement of equipment.

Telecommunications Mast

A typical example of a mast featuring cellular antenna and equipment such as RRUs (Remote Radio Heads), fiber cabling, and microwave dishes used for network backhaul linking.

Electrician

I have also considered a career as an electrician. This hands-on job is similarly themed to the others listed above as it involves installing, replacing, and testing cabling and its equipment, in a myriad of locations. It has led me to research and speak with local self-employed electricians as well as well-known national companies such as SSE.
